FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: Submit leave request through the HR portal at least 2 weeks before the intended leave date.

Q: Can I work remotely?
A: Yes, up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Q: What is the probation period?
A: 3 months for all new employees.

Q: When do I get paid?
A: Salary is paid on the last working day of each month.
